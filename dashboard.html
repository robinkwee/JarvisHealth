What is JarvisHealth?

JarvisHealth is a group food tracking app that helps you and your friends log meals instantly via Telegram. Just send a photo of your food, and the AI automatically calculates calories and macros.

  • Send food photos to Telegram group
  • Instant calorie & macro analysis
  • Track your daily targets
  • See your progress in real-time
  • Share the journey with friends

How it works:

  1. Join the JarvisHealth Telegram group
  2. Register with your email & Telegram ID
  3. Send a photo of your meal to the group
  4. AI analyzes the meal instantly
  5. Check your dashboard to see progress
