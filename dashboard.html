What is JarvisHealth?

JarvisHealth lets you and your friends track meals together using Telegram. Just send a photo of your food to the group chat — the AI instantly identifies the meal, estimates calories and macros, and logs it to your personal dashboard.

No manual entry. No app to download. Just snap and send.

  • Send food photos to a shared Telegram group
  • AI identifies the meal and calculates macros instantly
  • Each person tracks their own personal daily targets
  • View your progress on this dashboard anytime
  • Share the journey with your friends

To join: message the Telegram bot with /start to get your User ID, then register below.
